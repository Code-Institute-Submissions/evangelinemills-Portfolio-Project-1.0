About Me

Jane Doe RN/RM/IBCLC

My name is Jane Doe. I am a parcticing registered midwife in the South-East of Ireland. I qualified as a Nurse in 2006 and went on to do my postgrad in Midwifery in 2012. I am so passionate about my work and love providing newborns and thier families with the best start in life. In 2020, I was lucky enough to undertake a course in Lactation Consultancy and I am now registered with the International Board of Certified Lactation Consultants. I can provide you with the best up to date, evidence based care, to help you feel confident, and support you throughout this challenging time! As a mother myself I understand the struggles we face as new parents and the difficulties that newborn feeding can throw at us. With the right support, direction and knowledge, together we can kickstart your breastfeeding journey and give you and your newborn the best possible start in life!

What I Offer

Online Breastfeeding Preparation Class

This is a group class aimed to provide you and your partner with the essential knowledge needed to prepare you for breastfeeding. This class should be taken between 24 and 32 weeks gestation. It runs for 3 hours via zoom with a tea break in the middle. It is an opportunity to learn and ask questions and meet like-minded couples about to undertake the same journey as you!

1:1 Home Consultation

I can visit your home once you have arrived home with your newborn. This 2 hour vsit gives us an opportunity to chat about your expectations, overcome any issues you may be having and work on getting the best out of your breastfeeding adventure!

Online Consultation

Book and online consultation at a time that suits you! This 1:1 video call allows you to ask any questions or overcome any issues from the comfort of your own home. If you feel confident to apply knowledge and skills through this onlie channel then this is for you.

1:1 Tailored Class

This 1:1 tailored class is designed to teach you and your partner the basics in preparing for the arrival of your new baby.
