About Me

Claire Ferris RN/RM/IBCLC

I am an international board certified lactation consultant (IBCLC) midwife, nurse and mother. I have been helping mothers and babies to breastfeed since 2006. I have a qualification in Parenthood education and facilitate classes on breastfeeding topics to parents and other professionals. I have had 3 very different personal breastfeeding experiences which include breastfeeding after emergency cesarean section, feeding a premature baby and also breastfeeding after loss. I stay current with the latest research on lactation, infant nutrition and infant development so that I can provide parents and professionals with the most accurate information available. I can help with preparation for breastfeeding and getting off to a good start, overcoming issues whilst breastfeeding as well as intorducing solids, returning to work whilst breastfeeding or ending your breastfeeding journey. I am also able to help with pumping, use of nipple shields, harvesting colostrum, combination feeding and much much more!

What I Offer

Online Breastfeeding Preparation Class

This is a group class aimed to provide you and your partner with the essential knowledge needed to prepare you for breastfeeding your newborn. This class is taken antenatally. It runs via zoom with a structured format and Q&A session at theend. It is an opportunity to learn and ask questions and meet like-minded couples about to undertake the same journey as you! €60

1:1 Home Consultation

I visit you once you have given birth and returned home with your newborn. This vsit gives us an opportunity to chat about your expectations, overcome any issues you may be having and work on getting the best out of your breastfeeding adventure! €120

Online Consultation

Book an online consultation with me at a time that suits you! This 1:1 video call allows you to ask any questions or overcome any issues from the comfort of your own home. If you feel confident to apply knowledge and skills through this virtual channel then this is for you. I follow up with a phone call after a few days to ensure all is going well. €100

1:1 Tailored Class

This 1:1 tailored class is designed to teach you and your partner the basics in preparing for the arrival of your new baby in the privacy of your own home. During this visit we cover all things breastfeeding but it is tailored to your individual needs and wishes! €120
